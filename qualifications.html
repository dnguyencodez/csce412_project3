Cybersecurity

With almost all of our personal and business information being held by databases and networks, we are all prone to more data breaches. This is why I am interested in cybersecurity. Cybersecurity is the process of protecting data, systems, and networks from security attacks. It is important to keep our information secure from the unknown, and to keep everything personal within our own reach. Not only that, but many companies have access to and store our personal information, so it is also important to build strong cybersecurity departments to prevent hacks from occurring across the globe. There have already been large breaches within U.S. data, so that comes to show how important cybersecurity is in modern day.
